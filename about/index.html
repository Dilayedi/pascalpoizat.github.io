Currently


Since September 2012, I have a full professor position at Université Paris Ouest Nanterre la Défense (historically, Paris X), located in Nanterre, some kms away from La Défense business center, in the west of Paris. As far as research is concerned, I am a member of the LIP6 laboratory, which is the computer science laboratory of Université Pierre et Marie Curie (historically, Paris VI), located inside Paris.

Before


I did my Master studies at Université de Nantes (west of France) between 1993 and 1995, and obtained a research MSc in Computer Science in June 1995. After my National Service (compulsory at that time), I began in September 1997 a PhD in Computer Science still at Université de Nantes and at the IRIN (now LINA) laboratory. I defended this PhD in December 2000.

After a one-year temporary assistant professor position (ATER in French) at IUT de Nantes, I obtained in September 2001 an associate professor position at Université d'Evry Val d'Essonne (France), in the south of the Paris Metropolitan Area. I did research between 2001 and 2008 in the LaMI (now IBISC) laboratory in Evry and after two years at INRIA Paris-Rocquencourt I moved to the LRI laboratory in Orsay, also in the south of Paris. In 2011 I passed an "Habilitation à diriger des recherches" (what is this? see here) at Université Paris Sud in Orsay.
